From the Artist

September, 1996
The World of Copland Icon Collection: Vol. 1 by Gedeon Maheux & Corey Marion

The enclosed folders contain 40 icons in the style of Mac OS8, otherwise known as Copland. Since Copland was first revealed, there have been numerous folder based icon sets created. In creating The World of Copland, the Iconfactory has focused more on "every-day objects" instead of simple folders. Icons include media sets (video and audio casettes), computer components, household objects, and a few folders thrown in for good measure.

Thanks: We would like to say thank you to all of you who have written to let us know how much you like our work on the icons. Since we don't charge money for any of our stuff, it is very gratifying to get such a positive responses from everyone... thanks!
